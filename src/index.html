About me

I am 24 years old and I come from Ukraine. After school in 2015, I entered the Faculty of Telecommunications at the National Aviation University in Kyiv, but left the country before graduating. For a while I lived in Poland, Austria and Germany. Two years ago I settled in Slovenia in the city of Maribor. In 2021, I started looking for a suitable profession for me. I became interested in web design and while taking an online course I realized that I like programming more than designing. After that, I continued my learning in the field of web development. Currently I enjoy tha most working on projects where it is necessary to combine front-end with back-end, and specifically programming in Python.

I have traits such as consistency, accuracy and creativity. I love solving Japanese crossword puzzles and playing Sudoku, as well as performing on stage, writing scripts and making handicrafts. I think that the combination of such characteristics will help me become a good programmer.
